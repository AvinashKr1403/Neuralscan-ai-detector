The Dataset

HOW IT WORKS

The Method

01

Pattern Recognition

Input text is tokenized and scored against a library of linguistic fingerprints. The engine computes probability scores trained on AI and human writing samples to detect subtle patterns.

02

Heuristic Analysis

Sentence-length variance, filler phrase density, contraction frequency, casual voice patterns, and punctuation style are measured and weighted to build a secondary signal score.

03

Fusion Score

The final score combines both signals — 70% pattern recognition and 30% heuristic analysis. Texts scoring above 0.5 are classified as AI; below 0.5 as Human. Confidence reflects distance from the decision boundary.

04

Built by RavenBuilds
